Tomato Soup

Image of a tomato soup

Description

This is a nice lunch dish

Ingredients

  • 750g of plum tomatoes
  • Onion
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1 litre stock
  • Herbs - basil, oregano

Method

  1. Heat up oil then add diced onion
  2. After 5 mins add crushed garlic
  3. Add remaining ingredients
  4. Simmer for 20 mins then blend
